# Gravity

## Overview

Gravity is the force that attracts objects with mass toward each other.

In Cosmos Engine, gravity is currently modeled using Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation.

---

## Newton's Law

F = G * (m1 * m2) / r²

Where:

- F = gravitational force
- G = gravitational constant
- m1 = mass of body one
- m2 = mass of body two
- r = distance between bodies

---

## Effects of Gravity

Gravity causes:

- Orbital motion
- Planetary systems
- Satellite trajectories
- Tidal effects

---

## Current Implementation

Cosmos Engine currently uses a simplified simulation scale.

Units are not yet tied to real-world meters and kilograms.

Future versions may support realistic astronomical units.
