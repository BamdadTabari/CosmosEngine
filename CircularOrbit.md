# Circular Orbit

## Overview

A circular orbit occurs when an object moves around a central body at a constant distance.

The orbit eccentricity is zero.

---

## Orbital Velocity

v = √(μ / r)

Where:

- μ = standard gravitational parameter
- r = orbital radius

---

## Properties

- Constant altitude
- Constant speed
- Constant distance from central body

---

## In Cosmos Engine

Circular orbits are useful for testing:

- Gravity calculations
- Integrators
- Stable orbital motion
